## Product Selected
Smart AI Refrigerator

---

## Problem Statement
Users often forget stored food items, leading to expiry and food wastage. There is no smart tracking system to manage refrigerator contents efficiently.

---

## Design Thinking Process

### Empathize
- Users forget items in fridge  
- Busy lifestyle reduces tracking ability  
- Food expires unnoticed  

### Define
- Lack of intelligent tracking system  
- No expiry notifications  
- No visibility of stored items  

### Ideate
- AI-based inventory tracking  
- Mobile app integration  
- Barcode scanning system  
- Smart alerts and notifications  

### Prototype
- Smart fridge with camera system  
- App showing fridge inventory  
- Expiry reminder system  

### Test
- User feedback collection  
- Reduce food wastage measurement  
- UI/UX improvement
